Free image sites:

  • Pixabay - not only images, but musics, icons, videos, and all other resources to build a website!
  • Vecteezy - mainly images and videos.
  • Pexels - lots of images in good quality!
  • IconFinder - good site for icons, some are free and some are paid.

Tools to help with design and develop:

  • Webflow - the templates and the online designer tools are very good to use.
  • Lanhu - good teamwork designing ability.
  • Color Hunt - quickly get color matching inspiration, can find color matching based on keywords.

Good UI design - can be used as references:

  • Ant Design - flat design, simple and clear color matching, have good adaptability to different page sizes.
  • Material Design - published by Google, designed in Google and Android style.
